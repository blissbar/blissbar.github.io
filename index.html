ASMR Benefits

Gentle touch ASMR can evoke the following benefits:

  • Relaxation
  • Stress-reduction
  • Enhanced Focus
  • Sense of Connection
  • Mood Enhancement

Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response (ASMR) refers to a deeply pleasant, soothing, and sometimes sleepy feeling that occurs in response to distinct acoustics, visual stimuli, and personal attention.
